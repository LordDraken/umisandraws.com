Refund Policy

Refunds are only acceptable if for some reason *emergency or unforeseen circumstances* I cannot complete your commission. These will be rare and on a case by case basis. If you feel the work is unacceptable then message/mail me and we will work something out.

Payment Process

The Payment Amount is to be agreed upon before beginning the Commissioned Artwork. Once a request is made and agreed upon then full payment is expected up front.

Image Rights

The commissioned piece is for personal use only. It may be posted to the client's gallery and used in any way for personal, non-commercial use. The client understands in reading this, that legal action will proceed in the event of the following:

Umisan's signature is cropped or removed completely from the posted work.
The image is sold to a third party for any reason, as the client does not have that right.
The image is copied, redistributed, or re-posted anywhere without proper credit (signature remains in the image and a direct link to the Artist's FA page is in the description box) attributed to the creator

Timeframe

Completed Commissions should take no longer than 1 week from the commission date.
If complications arise notification shall occur ASAP

Resources

Please send specific details along with your request, such as: Pose references, color scheme references, and pictures of your OC (from different angles if possible), and any must-have details such as markings, piercings, and extra limbs (wings, horns, etc.). I will send you WIPs of my progress during each stage.

Drafting Process/Revisions

My work comes in 3 stages, Sketch, Line-art, and Color, if any changes need to be made to the Commission, 3 revisions are allowed per piece, per character (I.E. If you have 2 characters you are allowed 2 revisions per stage).

Content

I have the right to refuse any content that makes me uncomfortable (Hover over for Examples). Minor expections can be made on a case-by-case basis, including additional charge. Please inquire during commission process.

Vore, Scat, Gore, Water Sports, Rape/Non-Con, Hyper-Inflation, M-Preg, Female Genitalia/Intercourse, Feral Intercourse, Body Mutilation or any content that is implied to be of a racist, homophobic, bigoted, sexist, or otherwise offensive nature.

Customer Conduct

Commissioners are asked to communicate in a professional and appropriate manner. That is to communicate without the use of slurs or derogatory language, and to be respectful of my time and effort by promptly responding to questions and giving any and all references and information that help with the drawing. Any clients refusing to abide by this rule will be automatically rejected and will be blocked indefinitely. If this behavior starts once payment has been made a conduct warning will be issued. If the behavior continues the commission will be scrapped and any payments made will NOT be refunded.
